Overview

This programme from Sport England aims to fund around 500 playing fields across England to support participation and retention of people playing sport of the local level.

Who is Eligible?

Organisations in England that can demonstrate future management, maintenance and sporting use at the site along with the site being protected for 25 years or longer are welcome to apply. This means that organisations must own (or intend to own) the freehold or have a lease for the field for that length of time.

Projects which can offer protection of playing fields “in perpetuity” and projects where ownership is being transferred to the community from public ownership will be prioritised.

What will I get?

No minimum or maximum grant has been announced.

Funds will cover capital costs that improve playing fields for sporting and community use and help to protect the long term sporting purpose of the field. Work is likely to involve creating new pitches or levelling or drainage of existing ones.

Education establishments are expected to contribute 30% partnership funding. Partnership funding can be from your own funds, other grants, sponsorship and non-cash (in kind) contributions. All partner funding contributions must be evidenced in writing before any funding will be released.
