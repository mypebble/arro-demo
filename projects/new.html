The Exploration Deck

Project Completion Date - 05/06/2016

Our project is the creation of an Exploration Deck for the children that surround our school. The Exploration Deck will be used by teachers, care providers and parents to facilitate investigation and reflective time for children who suffer from autism and behavioural issues. It will provide stimulation to all types of learners allowing for differentiation and encouragement of personalised learning treating each child as an individual.
